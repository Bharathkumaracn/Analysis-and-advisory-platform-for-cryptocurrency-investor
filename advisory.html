The Idea:

Only predicting future value of a coin is not sufficient to decide when the best time is to buy or sell a coin. In addition to predicted value, other indicators like RSI, EMA and MACD are required to be considered. Technical analysis is based on the price reflecting all current information on an asset, and that the current price is the current perception of value. Also, technical analysis is used to isolate trending and non-trending periods. Technical indicators help in analysing the movement in the coin prices whether the ongoing trend is going to continue or reverse. But market indicators are new and are not so easy to understand. The Technical indicators used are:

• Relative Strength Index (RSI): The RSI is a momentum indicator used in technical analysis that measures the magnitude of recent price changes to evaluate overbought or oversold conditions in the price of an asset.
The RSI is displayed as an oscillator (a line graph that moves between two extremes) and can have a reading from 0 to 100.
• Exponential Moving Average (EMA): It is a type of moving average (MA) that places a greater weight and significance on the most recent data points. 20, 50 and 200 period EMA are most commonly used.
• Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD): It is a trend-following momentum indicator that shows the relationship between two moving averages of an asset price. MACD is represented as a histogram. Fundamentally, MACD helps traders detect when the recent momentum in a coin’s price may signal a change in its underlying trend. This can help traders decide when to enter, add to, or exit from a particular coin. This module considers the above technical indicators along with the price action analysis to decide whether the coins price is about to surge or not.
• How to enter a BUY position:
For long term BUY position wait for 50-day EMA to close above 100-day EMA.
For short term BUY position wait for 12-day EMA to close above 26-day EMA.
• How to enter a SELL position:
For long term SELL position wait for 50-day EMA to close below 100-day EMA.
For short term BUY position wait for 12-day EMA to close below 26-day EMA.
